Our advisors consist of >25 client-facing leaders in web3, working for industry-leading companies across CeFi, DeFi, SaaS, L1s & L2s, and Financial Institutions. They align with our mission to transform the crypto recruitment process to better serve the needs of companies looking to hire the best talent, by helping us source candidates at scale through their networks; they are truly the backbone of Revenue3, and enable us to unlock the following benefits for our clients:

Scalable Network Effect

Gain access to an extensive network of industry leaders who know, and work with qualified and relevant candidates that are not easily discoverable through traditional channels.

Industry leaders are better equipped to screen candidates than recruiters, ensuring only the best candidates make it through the initial stages of the recruitment process.
